About the System

Digitizing the college stationery experience for everyone.

Project Overview

College students often struggle to know which stationery items are available, their prices, and discounts before visiting the shop. Shopkeepers also face difficulty managing inventory manually, leading to stockouts or overstocking. This system provides a unified digital platform that helps manage stationery efficiently, bridging the gap between student needs and inventory management.

Our Objectives

Help students check stationery availability instantly.

Help shopkeepers manage inventory digitally and accurately.

Provide automatic low stock alerts to prevent shortages.

Improve efficiency and organization in college stationery stores.
